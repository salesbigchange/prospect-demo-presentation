The BigChange Journey

From a Visionary Idea to an All-in-One Powerhouse

The story of BigChange begins with Martin Port, a renowned entrepreneur in the Field Service Management sector. After founding and building Masternaut into one of Europe's largest vehicle tracking companies, he sold it to private equity in 2010.

Martin realised he'd spent his career integrating his tracking system into countless different CRM systems, job management platforms, and databases. He saw the inefficiency and frustration this caused for businesses.

This led to a powerful idea: Why not build a single, all-in-one system from the ground up? A native platform that seamlessly combines job management, CRM, and vehicle tracking.

That vision became BigChange.
